How it works

Step 1: Online Testing

Complete a suite of online psychometric tests.

Step 2: Reports circulated to both parents and students.

Parents recieve a full report which is discussed and the student is given access to the online platform.

Step 3: First in person meeting with student

1 hour meeting with the career coach to dicuss psychometric tesing, careers interest report and next steps.

Step 4: Follow up meeting with student and parents.

Second meeting with student and final meeting with parents to discuss options available to the student.
